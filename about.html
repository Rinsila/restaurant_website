ABOUT US

SafAyz is nestled in the heart of Calicut, our café is a cozy retreat for food enthusiasts seeking a perfect blend of flavors and a welcoming ambiance.
Step inside, and you'll find a warm and inviting atmosphere, adorned with rustic yet modern decor. The comfortable seating, dimmed lighting, and subtle background music create an ideal setting for casual gatherings, family outings, or even a quiet moment of solitude with your favorite indulgence.
Our menu is a culinary journey that spans the savory delights of burgers and pizzas to the sweet symphony of cakes. Bite into our succulent burgers, crafted with premium ingredients, from juicy beef patties to tender grilled chicken, all sandwiched between freshly baked buns. Whether you're a fan of classic flavors or crave something adventurous, our diverse burger menu caters to every taste bud.
For pizza aficionados, our chefs masterfully blend traditional and innovative toppings on a crispy crust to create a mouthwatering experience. From the timeless Margherita to signature gourmet combinations, each pizza is a masterpiece, crafted with passion and attention to detail.
And for those with a sweet tooth, our dessert selection is a showcase of heavenly cakes. Indulge in the velvety richness of chocolate, the lightness of a fruit-infused delight, or the nostalgia of a classic cheesecake. Our cakes are made with love and expertise, ensuring a perfect balance of sweetness in every slice.
